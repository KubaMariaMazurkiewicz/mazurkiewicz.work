Hello!

My name is Kuba Maria Mazurkiewicz. I am a designer and artist. I work at the Faculty of Artistic Research at the Academy of Fine Arts in Warsaw, where I teach perceptiveness, design, and an artistic approach to locality. I specialize in visual identity, book design, and exhibitions. I create my own art publications and exhibitions.
