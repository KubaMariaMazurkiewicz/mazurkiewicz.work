_Hidden Remains. Centrally Planned Housing of the Polish Countryside_ was an exhibition held at the Museum of Architecture in Wrocław from 4 December 2025 to 5 April 2026. Curated by the PROLOG studio in collaboration with the museum team, the exhibition explored the architecture of centrally planned farms in the Polish People’s Republic.
